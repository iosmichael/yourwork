Who We Are

YourWork is a technology-driven, car-maintenance company focused on providing a convenient and affordable alternative to typical auto shop maintenance.

Rather than taking your car into the shop with little idea about what is wrong with it, YourWork will provide an app on your phone that puts you in complete control of your car maintenance.

The YourWork app will connect, via a Bluetooth connection, directly to the diagnostic system of your car. It will then display the issue, the parts and tools needed, as well as the video instructions for you to fix it yourself or have a better idea of what you should be charged for the work.

This app is more than just another addition to the Google and Apple app stores; it is a complete transformation of the way that we look at fixing our cars.

FAQ

What is OBDII?

OBDII is an onboard diagnostic system that has been installed in almost every car and light truck since 1996. Practically speaking, it allows you to see every problem in a modern vehicle, provided that the affected region has an electronic connection to the system and you have the proper equipment.

How easy is it to use?

At the moment, fairly difficult. It is easy to purchase a reader that will pop out the code for the problem, but for the moment, there is no consolidated database of OBDII codes for the average consumer. That is why these codes are normally only used by mechanics and car salesman.

Why would I use YourWork?

How often do you go to the doctor to get both diagnosed and treated? What if you could know exactly why you are ill and then decide whether or not you want the doctor to treat you? If it’s a cold, something the doctor will only give you over the counter pharmaceuticals for, you stay at home and save yourself the time and the charge. If not, then you go to the doctor with full knowledge of what is wrong and the price of treating it. It’s the same thing here, only with cars. If you can fix it easily, cheaply, and quickly, then you do. If not, then you go to your local auto shop knowing exactly what you want and how expensive it is.

What if I do not have any place to work on my car?

Already, Do-It-Yourself garages with space and tools are opening in the US. Not only that, but our plan is to combine this technology with the actual auto shop for the everyday consumer. So for now, use a friend’s garage or find a DIY shop, and hopefully we will have our solution provided soon.
